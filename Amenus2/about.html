Bridging technology with human narrative to craft meaningful solutions.

A self-made entrepreneur, founder of a global CMMI Level 3 tech company, and a published author. I believe technology is a human story. How I Got Here

My path began as a fresh IT graduate and an introverted bookworm. A pivotal win in an inter-school dance competition sparked a transformation, teaching me the power of expression. This journey of self-discovery continued through grooming classes and a mentor who saw my potential as a communicator, pushing me into public speaking and sales roles. My fascination with International Relationship Management led me to found MaMo TechnoLabs LLP in 2014. Today, I wear many hats from Developer and Product MVP Specialist to Delivery Head and Public Speaker guiding 20+ international client accounts annually and turning technological vision into market-ready strategy.
